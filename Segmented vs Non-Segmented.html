Difference: Segmented vs Non-Segmented

Non-Segmented HTML:

  • Sab content ek hi container mein hota hai
  • Structure clear nahi hota
  • Small pages ke liye theek

Segmented HTML:

  • Page multiple logical parts mein divided hota hai
  • Header, nav, main, footer clearly defined hote hain
  • Real-world websites isi structure ko use karti hain
